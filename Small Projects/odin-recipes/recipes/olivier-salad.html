Olivier Salad

Description

This dish consists of boiled potato, tuna and mayo

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Canned Tuna

Steps

  1. First, we peel and boil the potatoes.
  2. Secondly, we boil the eggs.
  3. Then, we will proceed to chop all potatoes and eggs into a bowl.
  4. Add the tuna to the bowl.
  5. And finally, add the mayo and mix everything.
  6. Be sure to mash the potatoes a bit (not too much) for different textures.
