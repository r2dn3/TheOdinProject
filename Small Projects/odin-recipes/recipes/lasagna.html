Lasgna

Description

This dish consists of bologna, pasta, meat and béchamel.

It is prepared in the oven over a long period.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta Noodles
  • Minced Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Celery
  • Bechamel sauce

Steps

  1. First, cook the minced meat along with the carrots, onion and celery in a non-stick pan.
  2. Once cooked through, add the tomato sauce and keep in low heat for an hour or so.
  3. Add salt and pepper to the sauce.
  4. Once the sauce is finished, boil the pasta noodles.
  5. Heat up or make the béchamel sauce.
  6. In a big, oven-ready recipient, we will begin mounting our lasagna:
  7. First, bologna sauce. Then pasta noodles, then béchamel sauce. Repeat this step three times.
  8. Once mounted, add some parmisan or mozzarela cheese on top.
  9. Put in the oven at 180c for 2 hours.
  10. Once finished, let it rest for half an hours while covered.
